From the founder Why I started FitCo

I used to buy pants by waist size, because that's what everyone does. They fit my waist, but everything else looked off.

Some squeezed my thighs
Some had too much room in the seat
Others made my legs look shorter

I stopped asking "What size am I?" and started asking "What cut was this pair actually made for?"

That changed how I bought pants. FitCo is my way of making it easier for everyone else.

The problem

Why pants fit so badly

Most men buy pants by waist size. It doesn't tell you how they fit everywhere else.

Thigh Taper Inseam Proportions

Tight in the thighs

Your waist fits but your thighs don't. Common for athletic builds.

Waist gaps

Your hips fit but the back gapes open. Most brands don't account for this.

Wrong length

Inseam varies by brand, cut, and fabric. Most men settle for hemming what should have fit.
