Why FitCo exists

I've spent years buying pants that technically fit and still looked wrong. Tight through the thighs, no room in the seat, or a waist I could fit a fist behind. Sizing up fixed one problem and created another. Eventually I realized the sizing system just wasn't built for how bodies actually vary. FitCo is the shortcut I wish I'd had: the handful of pants I'd genuinely tell a friend to buy, instead of hundreds that probably won't fit.
